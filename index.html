Built for daily document work

The controls teams need. Nothing ornamental.

CliqSign covers the practical details that keep agreements moving and make the final record useful.

Smart field detection

Identify likely signature, initials, date, text, email and checkbox locations from labels, blanks and PDF form fields.

Reusable templates

Save prepared PDFs with standard fields, browse by category and start repeat agreements without rebuilding the workflow.

Custom invitations

Adjust the subject, message and CC list, preview the email and insert signer, document, sender and expiry details.

Reminders and expiry

Prompt outstanding email recipients automatically and set a clear deadline for every signing request.

Signer passcodes

Add an access code for individual recipients, generate one in the workflow and reset it while signing is pending.

Document controls

Void, expire, extend, duplicate or edit and resend documents while preserving clear lifecycle behaviour.

Custom branding

Apply your logo, colour theme and business identity to signing pages and emails, with signed-PDF footer controls.

Team workspaces

Separate personal and team documents, invite collaborators and assign Admin, Member or Viewer access.

Signed webhooks

Deliver selected document and signer events as JSON payloads with HMAC-SHA256 signature verification.
